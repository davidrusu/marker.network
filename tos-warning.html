A quick note before we get started

The reMarkable Cloud service provided by reMarkable AS comes with some terms and conditions that seems to suggest that accessing your files from the reMarkable Cloud through third party software may lead to losing access to the reMarkable Cloud.

This software has been carefully designed[1] to preserve your privacy as well as interact respectfully with the reMarkable Cloud.

We imagine the terms and conditions are designed to protect reMarkable Cloud from badly designed software, so we hope to have mitigated some risk there.

With all that said, we can't make any guarantees, thus we want you to know that you may be violating reMarkable Cloud terms and conditions by using this software.

reMarkable AS. Terms & Conditions
[1] We keep your device token safely on your machine and cache your notebooks in-order to put minimal load on reMarkable Cloud.
