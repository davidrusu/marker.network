Link Your Device

We need to link this app with your reMarkable tablet through the reMarkable's cloud.

  1. Login to your reMarkable Cloud account https://my.remarkable.com
  2. Then generate a new one-time-code (OTC) by visiting https://my.remarkable.com/connect/desktop
  3. Copy the OTC into the box below and hit register

A NOTE ON PRIVACY: This software is designed to keep your notes safe and secure. Only material directly related to your site is uploaded to marker.network. Nothing else.

The device token generated from this one-time-code is stored on your computer and only used to authenticate with reMarkable AC.
