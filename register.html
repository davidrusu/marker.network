Link Your Device

To link your tablet, generate a new one-time-code (OTC) and enter it below: https://my.remarkable.com/device/desktop/connect

NOTE ON PRIVACY

The device token generated from this one-time-code is stored on your computer and only used to authenticate with reMarkable AC. It is never shared with marker.network or any other third parties.
